Junior Software Developer

Hailing from Washington D.C originally, I have lived in Indiana for over fifteen years. Having spent the majority of my professional life in the culinary arts, I have experienced many high-paced, high pressure job experiences, leaving me with strong teamwork and leadership skills. I am hoping to transfer these skills to a new field, as my knowledge and skills in software developement grow. I hope that I will find in coding the same rewarding challenges in critical thinking and problem solving that I found in the culinary world.

In my time at ElevenFifty Academy I have become familiar with C# and HTML/CSS. One example project utilizing all three, is a live music journal app called SongBook. SongBook stores information about live musical performaces you have seen. Info about the band, the venue, and the songs that were played, is stored and organized.
